View Our Timeline

1930

1930s period between the two world wars was an era of growth and financial progress of Karachi. The era provided a golden opportunity to keep MEK abreast with the technical know-how of that time and to expand its manufacturing facilities.

1950

MEK manufacturing plant located at Lyari, Karachi.

1951

Mulla Ebrahimji Karimbhoy, the founding father of MEK. Picture taken in 1951 by his son Mohsin MEK.

1955

MEK factory was located at Ireland Road Karachi in a rented warehouse. Mohsin bhai is seen with a group of manufacturing staff.

1962

MEK participated in many expos and exhibitions to showcase the product lines being manufactured. The picture is of expo held during the golden jubilee celebration of our holiness Syedna Tahir Saifuddin at Tayyabi Jamaatkhana, Karachi in 1962.

1970

In the mid 1960s, after the retirement of Mulla Ebrahimji Karimbhoy, his son Mohsin bhai took over the reins of the company. during Mohsin bhais era, new manufacturing facility was developed at Lyari. New products were launched such as safe deposit lockers and strong room doors that were earlier imported from the united kingdom and India. In the picture taken in 1970s, Mohsin MEK is seen sitting in his office. the show room was than located at Serai road.

1980

Manufacturing facilities were expanded with the shifting to the larger company-owned factory in Lyari, Karachi

2013

The growing law and order problems in Lyari compelled the company to develop a new manufacturing facility at Korangi creek Industrial park, Karachi. The construction and development of this new facility started in 2013 and the manufacturing of products started in 2017.

2017

The launching of this new facility also coincided with the changing of guards. Forth generation was successfully incorporated in the management of company who are contributing and leading the growth of MEK towards the new horizon.
